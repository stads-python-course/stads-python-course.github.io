While not exam-relevant, I highly recommend exploring Plotly, especially Plotly Express, in addition to Matplotlib for data visualization. Plotly offers interactive and visually engaging charts that enhance data exploration. Plotly Express simplifies complex plotting tasks, providing a quick and powerful way to create beautiful visualizations with minimal code (not as technical as Matplotlib).
